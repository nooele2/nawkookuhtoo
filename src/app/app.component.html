About Me

Builder by necessity,
developer by choice.

I'm originally from Myanmar. When the military coup in 2021 upended life as I knew it, I had to leave — and I found myself starting over in Chiang Rai, Thailand. That kind of reset forces you to figure out who you actually are.

I started eliminating things I didn't enjoy until tech was what was left. And when I started building — really building — something clicked. It was interesting. I kept going. That's still why I do it.

"I go where others don't. In life, in travel — and in the problems I choose to solve."

I'm an adventurous person by nature. I explore restricted areas, take risks, and do things most people wouldn't. That same restlessness pushes me in tech — I'm not interested in building another todo app. I want to build things that actually fix broken systems for real people in real places. My internship showed me exactly what that looks like: replacing a clunky Google Sheets bell schedule with a React & Python IoT system wired to actual hardware — because a school needed it to just work.

Vision

To build in Silicon Valley — and prove that where you start doesn't limit where you can go.

Mission
